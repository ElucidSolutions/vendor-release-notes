Make “Re-representation of Business Type” Modification Visible At All times

eMod

Update/Enhancement

Why was this changed?

In eMod, you, as a vendor, would only see the Re-representation of Business Type modification when there a change made to your vendor record in SAM. The original functinoary did not allow for all possible scenarios. As a result, some vendors have had difficulty keeping their contracts-up-to-date.

How does this affect eMod?

eMod will now give you the option to initiate a Re-representation of Business Type modification at all times. Options will be based upon your businessâ€™s records within SAM. Vendors will also have the ability to request that a small business type be deleted.

Refer to the eOffer/eMod User Guide for more information on executing the Re-representation of Business Type modification.
